Three design alternatives for the kettle-shopping task

Each prototype is shown as three mobile screens. Same starting context: shopping for a 1.5L electric kettle under $40 on an Omni-Mart app. Pick a prototype to view.

Static mid-fidelity mockups for survey evaluation. Side-by-side scoring uses identical phone framing across all three to avoid fidelity bias.
