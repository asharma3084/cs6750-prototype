How to use: Tap Filter, pick one option from each of the four groups, then Apply. The filter strip appears with chips. Tap an individual chip's × to remove that filter, or tap Clear all on the right end of the strip to wipe everything. Tap any product to open the product page - then tap back; filters stay. If a combination has no results, one chip fades grey and an orange card explains why; tap the grey chip for the recovery modal. The bottom nav (Home/Categories/Search/Cart/Account) is intentionally out of scope for this prototype - tapping shows a notice.
